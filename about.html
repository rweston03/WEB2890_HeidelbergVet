About Us

Heidelberg Veterinary Clinic has cared for pets in Heidelberg and the surrounding suburbs for over 30 years under the ownership and management of Dr. Rene French. Dr. French has recently retired and left the hospital in the capable hands of Dr. Dylan Barber, who is committed to continuing HVC's tradition of providing the best care money can buy to the pets of Heidelberg.
